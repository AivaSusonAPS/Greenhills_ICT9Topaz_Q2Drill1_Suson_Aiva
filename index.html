Cryptocurrencies of the World

Bitcoin (BTC)

- The first and most popular cryptocurrency, created in 2009 by an unknown person using the name Satoshi Nakamoto.


Ethereum (ETH)

- A decentralized platform that enables smart contracts and decentralized applications (DApps) to run without downtime or fraud.


BINANCE COIN (BNB)

- Used to pay fees on the Binance Exchange, BNB also powers the Binance Smart Chain ecosystem.

- Bring a changr of clothes to stay warm and dry during an evacuation. Designed for fast and low-cost international money transfers used by many banks and payment networks.
